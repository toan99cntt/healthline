About us
You’re on a journey to health and wellness. And we’re right there with you. How you feel affects every single day of your life, which is why you work so hard to get well and stay well. No matter your journey, we’re here to support, guide, and inspire you. We cut through the confusion with straightforward, expert-reviewed, person-first experiences — all designed to help you make the best decisions for yourself and the people you love. We want to help you own your well-being. Healthline covers all facets of physical and mental health openly and objectively because we’re here for the whole person — for your whole life. And because people come to us with unique challenges, we listen closely and put empathy first across our organization. In everything from our personal perspectives to our commitment to inclusivity, you’ll see that we feel with you. We hope you tap into all of Healthline’s resources — from our health and wellness library and newsletters to our apps, podcasts, and communities. Thanks for being here, The Healthline Team

Health seekers have made us the fastest growing health information site. Over 150 million people turn to Healthline every month.

Whether you’re here to learn more about a health condition, research a medication, tap into one of our communities, or get some tips for a healthier lifestyle, you’re in the right place. You’ll find content that’s informative, easy to understand, and engaging. You’ll also find a compassionate team of professionals who genuinely care about people. Ad & Sponsorship Policy

Healthline receives funding from advertisements, sponsored content, and other partnerships, such as affiliate programs. The reality is that funding from our advertisers allows us to cover more conditions and provide more powerful experiences, including video, animations, and input from a range of experts. Be assured that we’re absolutely inflexible about our editorial autonomy. Other than input about broad topic areas, we never allow sponsors to influence the content we create. We have specific ad and sponsorship guidelines we follow to ensure that advertising never gets in the way of editorial integrity. Healthline chooses the ads, does not endorse any products, distinguishes all ads from editorial content, and clearly distinguishes between sponsored and non-sponsored content.
Read more about our Ad & Sponsorship Policy.

Privacy Policy

Healthline is dedicated to protecting the privacy of readers and customers. Because customer privacy is our top priority, we proactively embed privacy into the development and curation of our services. At any time, all customers — regardless of geographical location — can make a data subject access request by using our rights request form. In doing so, customers can access, delete, restrict, or correct all personal information that they have shared with Healthline. Healthline will share your personal information with third-party marketers only after you have provided explicit consent to do so.

How we use Artificial Intelligence

At Healthline, we are committed to upholding the highest level of integrity to bring you the most accurate information on your health and wellness journey. We follow strict medical and editorial processes grounded in trustworthy, accessible, and accurate information. In some cases, Artificial Intelligence (AI), or similar technology, may be used in developing elements of our content. We have established an AI Editorial Process to ensure that any content created with the assistance of AI follows our rigorous medical and editorial process and aligns with our principles regarding the ethical use of AI included below.

Principles Regarding the Ethical Use of AI

  • Rigorous Vetting: All proposed uses of AI are rigorously vetted based on an established set of criteria so that all AI tools and technology are unbiased, reputable, and trustworthy.
  • Human Component: Healthline is committed to utilizing human expertise as the final medical and editorial approval stamp before publishing. No content will go straight from an AI tool or technology to publication without thorough human validation checks.
  • Rigorous Vetting: AI may be used to support and enhance editorial elements. We strive to be transparent about the use of AI in editorial copy.
We’re working hard to continually improve how we create the most accurate, relevant, inclusive, and actionable health information and products, so we want to hear from you if we could be doing better. If you have any questions or comments about the accuracy or usability of our content or feel an article is outdated, you can easily let us know by visiting this page.
